About Us

Losing a valuable document or property can be really frustrating. in some instances it has resulted to people
becoming victims of fraudelent activities
The Lost and Found center is determined to provide a lasting solution to this menace.
it provides a platform to register lost documents and properties and work hand in hand with the law enforcement agency to curb
malicious practices incase one loses an important document or property
Lost and found is designed in a way that is secure as users have to undergo authentification before they can provide any information to us.
Lost and Found is by far the best way to unite users with their Lost documents and property.
